About me

I’m Wing Pang, a Sydney based illustrator & designer with experience in both 2D and 3D animation. Currently, I'm working as a Junior UI/UX Designer for BarNet and a Contract Graphic Designer for SBS Australia. Previously, I worked as a Senior Visual Content Designer for Atomi Education

In 2021, I began studying a double degree of Bachelor of Design in Visual Communications and a Bachelor of Arts in International Studies (Japanese major) at the University of Technology Sydney.

In my free time, I enjoy journalling, playing with new technology and learning to code (this website was designed and coded from scratch using Adobe XD, HTML, CSS and Javascript!). I'm currently trying to improve my skills in JS and to pick up front-end development libraries.

Drawing and design has been my joy and passion since young and I hope that my works can invoke happiness for you too!
