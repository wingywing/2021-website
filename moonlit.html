Moonlit

2022 
comic

Overview

A digitally-illustrated vertical scrolling comic conveying the warmth of friendship despite a cold winter night at La Perouse. A whimsical journey of wide-eyed youth gathering to see the longest blood moon eclipse of the century, and an astronomically rare event that occurs only once every 25,000 years.
